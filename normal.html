What is a Cat Capsule?

About Cat Capsules

★ Cat Capsules
→ Normal Tickets can be used to collect one random item/upgrade per ticket used.
※ Normal Tickets can be gained as a daily login bonus or as rewards for completing special stages.

★ After drawing a capsule, you can choose to either use your prize immediately, store it, or convert it to XP.
Selecting "Use" will take you to the upgrade menu to confirm that you wish to upgrade the ability or unit.
Selecting "XP" will offer you an amount of XP equal to the value of the capsule.
Selecting "OK" will place the capsule in storage for later use.

About Cat Capsule Storage

Save items you receive for later use.
You can view the items in your storage at any time by tapping the button at the bottom left of the Capsule menu.
By tapping any capsule, you can choose toUse or Convert to XPthe rewards that you have received.

*If your Storage reaches maximum capacity, you will become unable to use the Capsule Machine until enough space is available.

*If you are unable to use the Capsule Machine, please confirm that you are in an area with a strong network/WiFi connection and try again.
